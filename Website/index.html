About BITS F.C. and our Mission

We are BITS F.C., the football team of BITS Pilani, K. K. Birla Goa Campus.
The inception of this semi-professional outfit was in 2009, when Shray Bansal pushed for participation in the 3rd division professional league under the Goa Football Association.

    In the 2016-17 season, we have participated in the Reliance Foundation Youth Sports - College Boys Football Tournament (Goa) and the 3rd Division League (Mormugao Zone) under the Goa Football Association.

    Future tournaments include Arena 2017, 1st All Goa Invitational Inter-Collegiate Football Tournament and Spree 2017.
